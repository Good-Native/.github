## Bloom — Pre-order & Notify Me for Shopify

<a href="https://www.goodnative.co/bloom">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Good-Native/.github/main/profile/img/bloom-logo.png" height="34" alt="Bloom — pre-order and Notify Me app for Shopify">
</a>

**Turn out-of-stock moments into future sales.**

<img align="right" width="340" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Good-Native/.github/main/profile/img/bloom-pdp.png" alt="Bloom pre-order and Notify Me on Shopify product pages — Join Waitlist and Pre-order examples">

[Bloom](https://www.goodnative.co/bloom) is a Built for Shopify **pre-order** and **Notify Me** (back-in-stock) app that works at the variant level — installed via app embed in any Shopify 2.0 theme, **without editing a single theme file**. Setup takes under 10 minutes.

- **Pre-orders per variant** — go-live schedules, oversell limits, and availability control
- **Notify Me demand capture** — automated back-in-stock flows via Klaviyo (email/SMS)
- **Clear customer expectations** — delivery messaging under Add to Cart, through cart, checkout, and order confirmation
- **Matches your brand** — buttons, messaging, fonts, colours, and spacing, with predefined style sets
- **Built for reporting** — orders and line items tagged with pre-order, so you can monitor demand and optimise inventory ordering

→ [Get early access](https://www.goodnative.co/bloom) · [Bloom help docs](https://help.goodnative.co)

## Hover — Site Health Checker

<a href="https://www.goodnative.co/hover">
  <picture>
    <source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Good-Native/.github/main/profile/img/hover-logo.png">

</a>

**Find problems before they cost you sales.**

<img align="right" width="340" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Good-Native/.github/main/profile/img/hover-metrics.png" alt="Hover site health dashboard — cache performance, response times, and broken link reports">

[Hover](https://www.goodnative.co/hover) monitors your site's health — catching broken links and performance issues early, identifying slow pages, highlighting pages running without cache, and surfacing issues impacting SEO, paid media, and conversion. Integrates with Webflow, with scheduled crawls and publish-triggered cache warming.
